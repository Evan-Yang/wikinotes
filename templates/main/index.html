A free and open source wiki resource for the courses at McGill university.

Find a course.

Enter a course name in the box in the top right corner, or browse our selection of courses:

  • Browse by faculty
  • Browse by department
  • Browse by professor
  • View popular courses

Learn and study.

Catch up on lectures you missed with lecture notes or course summaries. Quiz yourself with our multiple choice or vocabulary quizzes. Do a past exam and compare your answers to the student-made or official solutions. Read course reviews and tips written by other students.

Contribute.

Give back to the community by adding lecture notes, editing content or even just spreading the word. If you're interested in contributing or becoming a moderator, please register and check out our contributor guidelines.
